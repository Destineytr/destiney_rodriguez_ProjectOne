here are three of my favorite artists

javier marin

One of my favorite sculptors is Javier Marin, he is a Mexican artist known for his massive human form sculptures. He can make something that's been done a million times into something that is new and modern.

guillermo lorca garcia

Guillermo Lorca Garcia is a Chilean painter who seems to be largely influenced by baroque and renaissance art. He is a contemporary artist that creates larger than life oil paintings that look like they could be hundreds of years old.

bad bunny aka benito antonio

I'm a fan of Bad Bunny, a Puerto Rican singer an activist, because he is often trying to find the new ways to create music that resonates with millions while also talking about the struggles of Puerto Ricans and the diaspora.
